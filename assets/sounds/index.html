    sndJump

    Plays when Samus jumps straight up.

    sndHurt

    Plays when Samus gets hurt.

    sndBallBounce

    Plays when Samus bounces on the ground as Morph Ball.

    sndSpinJump

    Plays when Samus does a spinjump without Space Jump and Screw Attack.

    sndSpaceJump

    Plays when Samus does a spinjump with Space Jump but without Screw Attack.

    sndScrewAttack

    Plays when Samus does a spinjump without Space Jump but with Screw Attack.

    sndSpaceScrewAttack

    Plays when Samus does a spinjump with Space Jump and Screw Attack.

    sndBrake

    Plays when Samus brakes from a speedboost.

    sndGrab

    Plays when Samus clings on a wall using Power Grip.
    Also used when stepping on oFloorButton1, an unused leftover object from Confrontation.

    sndPullUp

    Plays when Samus climbs a wall using Power Grip.

    sndMSwitch

    Related to weapon (Missile, Supers, Power Bombs) switching.
    In 1.1, it played both when toggling between weapons, and when selecting them, in Toggle and Hold configurations. In the Community updates, it plays when toggling between weapons in the Toggle configuration, or when selecting a weapon in the Hold configuration.

    sndWallJump

    Plays when Walljumping off a wall.

    sndCrouch

    Plays when Samus crouches.

    sndMorph

    Plays when Samus morphs into a Morph Ball.

    sndUnMorph

    Plays when Samus unmorphs from a Morph Ball.

    sndHPickup

    Plays when collecting a small health drop. Also plays in 1.1 when collecting a big health drop.

    sndMPickup

    Plays when collecting a missile drop. Also plays in 1.1 when collecting a super missile drop.

    sndSBStart

    Plays when Speedbooster gets initiated.

    sndSBLoop

    Plays as a loop during Speedbooster.

    sndSBChargeLoop

    Plays as a loop after the Speedbooster has been charged via crouching.

    sndSJStart

    Plays when a shinespark gets initiated. The SJ stands for “Superjump”.

    sndSJLoop

    Plays during shinesparking.

    sndSBallStart

    Plays when activating Spider Ball.

    sndSBallLoop

    Plays as a loop during Spider Ball.

    sndLavaLoop

    Plays while taking damage under lava.

    sndPlantLoop

    Plays while taking damage from Plants.

    sndDrainLoop

    Plays while getting health drained from Larva Metroids or the Metroid Queen.

    sndMessage

    Plays while collecting minor tanks without the cutscene.

    sndWaterSplash

    Plays when Samus enters water.

    sndWaterExit

    Plays when Samus exits water. Also when Enemies enter or exit water.

    sndSave

    Plays when saving at a Save station.

    sndPlayerDeath

    Plays when Samus dies.
